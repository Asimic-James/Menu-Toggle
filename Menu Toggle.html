Code of Conduct

101. Every customer shall be treated with equity irrespective of their gender, tribe and age.

102. For every transaction carried out by a sales personnel, there shall be a recorded detail of such transaction.

103 (a). A customer must verify that a prospective goods/services are to his/her best satisfaction before any payment.

103 (b). If peradventure a transaction occurred and a customer observed some faults from the goods/merchandize. Such goods are entitled for a return within 24hrs of purchase, otherwise it shall be voided after 24hrs.

104 (a). An installmental payment of goods are welcomed, provided the goods are being reserved by the sales personnel until the full payment are made for collection.

104 (b). If peradventure a debt from a customer should occur, such debt must not exceed 48hrs, to avoid harassment from the sales personnel.

105. Every customer is entitled to air their views for an optimal service, as such views shall be treated with courtesy.
